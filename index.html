About Eau De Marithe

At Eau De Marithe, every bottle is a curated reflection of contemporary elegance. Our fragrances are crafted from luminous citrus top notes, rich floral hearts, and grounding woody or amber bases to create layered scent compositions that feel both modern and timeless.

We design each perfume to be an olfactory signature for those who appreciate artisanal quality, effortless glamour, and memorable fragrance experiences you can wear from sunrise to evening.
